Welcome to Böxle

Introducing Böxle – a mobile ledge designed for skateboarding. It stands at 25cm high, 35cm wide, and is approximately 180cm long. Böxle can be disassembled into three parts, making it easy to pack into a Eurobox (60x40x30).
